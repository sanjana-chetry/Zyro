# Zyro 🤖

AI-Powered Insurance Assistant

Zyro is an AI-powered assistant designed to make insurance easier to understand through conversational AI and interactive experiences. Instead of navigating complex policy documents, users can interact with Zyro to learn about insurance, ask questions, and even play quiz-based games.

This project was developed during the **Bajaj Hackathon**, exploring how AI can improve user engagement and accessibility in insurance platforms.

---

## 🚀 Features

*  **AI Chat Assistant** – Ask questions about insurance and receive simple, conversational explanations.
*  **Gamified Quiz Game** – Learn insurance concepts through fun quiz challenges.
*  **Reward Points System** – Earn points for answering quiz questions correctly.
*  **Voice Interaction** – Users can communicate with Zyro using voice input.
*  **Multi-Language Support** – Available in **English, Hindi, and Assamese** for broader accessibility.
*  **Advisor Mode for Agents** – Helps insurance agents with:

  * Sales guidance and tips
  * Explaining policies to customers
  * Comparing insurance plans easily
*  **Interactive UI** – Friendly interface featuring **Zyro**, the AI assistant with an engaging welcome screen.

---

##  How It Works

1. Users open the platform and are greeted by **Zyro**, the AI assistant.
2. Users can ask questions about insurance through chat or voice.
3. Zyro explains insurance concepts in a simple and understandable way.
4. Users can play quiz games to test their knowledge.
5. Correct answers reward users with **points**, making the learning experience interactive.
6. Insurance agents can switch to **Advisor Mode** to receive sales tips and compare insurance plans.

---

##  AI Integration

Zyro uses the **Llama 3.3 70B language model** through the **Groq API** to generate intelligent conversational responses for insurance-related queries.
